Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
1 yellow onion, sliced
3 tablespoons of olive oil
1 teaspoon of chili powder
1 teaspoon of cumin
1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
Salt and pepper, to taste
8 small flour tortillas
Toppings of your choice (e.g. shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, guacamole)
Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

In a large bowl, combine the chicken strips, sliced bell peppers, and sliced onion.

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.

Pour the spice mixture over the chicken and vegetables and toss to coat.

Spread the chicken and vegetables out on a large sheet pan and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

Warm the tortillas in the oven or microwave.

Serve the fajitas on the warm tortillas with your choice of toppings. Enjoy!
